Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a taxi from Bolton to Manchester Airport?

Do you provide airport transfers from Wigan?

Yes. We operate airport transfers from Wigan, Bolton and surrounding areas to both Manchester and Liverpool airports.

Are your drivers DBS checked?

Absolutely. All of our drivers are fully DBS checked and licensed for your safety and peace of mind.

Do you offer return journeys?

Yes, we recommend booking your return journey in advance to guarantee availability and secure your fixed return price.

Do you provide child seats or booster seats?

Yes. We provide child and booster seats free of charge upon request. Please select this option when making your booking.

Can I pay by card?

Yes. We accept cash, debit/credit cards and contactless payments. Corporate customers may also request invoicing.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We monitor all incoming flights using live flight tracking. If your flight is delayed, your driver will automatically adjust the pickup time at no extra cost.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes. You can book on behalf of a family member, friend, or business client. Just make sure to include their contact number and flight details.

What vehicles do you use?

We operate modern, comfortable vehicles including saloons, MPVs and 8-seat minibuses, suitable for business and family travel.

Do you offer corporate or business accounts?

Yes. We work with corporate clients and can arrange invoiced travel and regular transfers. Contact us to set up a business account.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, particularly during busy travel periods. Same-day bookings are accepted subject to availability.
