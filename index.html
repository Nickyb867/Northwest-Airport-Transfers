About Northwest Airport Transfers

Northwest Airport Transfers is your trusted provider of airport taxis from Bolton and Wigan to Manchester Airport (MAN) and Liverpool John Lennon Airport (LPL). We specialise in comfortable, reliable, and affordable transfers for both holidaymakers and business travellers.

All of our drivers are DBS checked and experienced, with excellent local knowledge of Bolton, Wigan, and the surrounding areas. Whether you need a Bolton taxi to Manchester Airport or a Wigan airport transfer to Liverpool Airport, our modern fleet of saloons and 8-seat minibuses ensures a smooth and stress-free journey every time.

Our Airport Transfer Services

  • Bolton to Manchester Airport Taxi – direct transfers with flight tracking and fixed prices.
  • Wigan to Manchester Airport Transfers – reliable, on-time service 24/7.
  • Bolton to Liverpool Airport Taxi – comfortable journeys from Bolton to LPL for individuals or groups.
  • Wigan to Liverpool Airport Transfers – affordable minibus and saloon taxis for family holidays or business travel.
  • Modern fleet: saloon cars, executive cars, and 8-seat minibuses.
  • DBS-checked, professional drivers with outstanding local knowledge.
  • 24/7 availability with fixed prices and no hidden fees.
  • Child & booster seats available upon request (no extra charge).
