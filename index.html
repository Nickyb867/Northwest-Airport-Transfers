Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

Use the form above and complete all steps, or call us on 07900 123456. You’ll receive a confirmation by email or text.

Do you provide child or booster seats?

Yes — choose “Child seat” or “Booster seat” in the booking form. Please include your child’s age so we can bring the right seat.

Can I get a receipt for business travel?

Absolutely. We can email a VAT-style invoice/receipt after your trip. Corporate accounts are available on request.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash or card. For card payments, let us know in advance or pay via a secure link we send before pickup.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We monitor flights for delays and adjust pickup times accordingly. Please provide your flight number in the notes.

How many passengers and bags can you take?

Our minibuses seat up to 8 passengers with ample luggage space. If you have extra-large items, mention this in the notes.
